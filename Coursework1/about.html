Dan Castellaneta


Dan Castellaneta is the man behind the voice of one of the most beloved animated characters Homer Simpson. He has also played other characters within the Simpsons like: Sideshow Mel, Mayor Quimby and Krusty the clown. Having been in the majority of the Simpsons television series he is well experienced the voice of Homer Simpson and has brought immense life to this character. Within the Simpsons Movie Homer, who is voiced by Dan, plays a big role in what happens to Springfield, in fact he is the reason why springfield becomes closed out from the rest of the world.












Nancy Cartwright


Nancy Cartwright is THE voice of Bart Simpson the 10-year-old boy who’s catch phrase “Eat my Shorts” has become well recognised. She has voiced other characters like Nelson Muntz, Ralph Wiggum and Todd Flanders but Bart is still the most recognisable voice out of them. She has even been an Emmy Award- winning actress for her “Outstanding Voice-Over Performance” in 1992 for her performance as Bart Simpson. The Simpsons Movie gives a very funny and exciting personality to the characters, especially Bart, where he will find himself in some incredibly awkward and possible uncomfortable positions.












Hank Azaria


Hank Azaria the voice of Simpsons characters like Moe Szyslak, Apu Nahasapeemapetilon, Chief Wiggum and Comic book guy. He has been voicing characters in the Simpsons since 1989 – present which has included many seasons and even the Simpsons hit and run game alongside Dan and Nancy. Within this movie he plays all the characters above and he takes on an important role with Moe, Apu and others including leading a riot to a particular family in Springfield and the soon to be famous line "Claw at it".
